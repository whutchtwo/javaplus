Resources to make compliance easy

The mission of Compliance Label Service is to save you time and money in achieving DoD and GSA compliance. That's why we created this extensive Learning Center. It will help decipher the RFID and Container label regulations of MIL-STD-129 and the UID marking regulations of MIL-STD-130, as well as their accompanying Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement (DFARS). It will also assist your understanding of ASN's, WAWF and Military Packaging. The Compliance Label Service Learning Center will even help you 'speak DoD' - see Glossary of terms and Military Alphabet. If you have any additional questions, please contact us.
