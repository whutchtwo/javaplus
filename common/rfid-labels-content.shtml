Military Shipping Label (MSL)

This is DoD's address label. MIL-STD-129 requires one on every:

  • Pallet
  • Individual box (i.e. non palletized shipping container)

EXPEDITER™ offers 3 MIL-STD-129 Military Shipping Label options:

  1. 4" x 6" MSL

    Thermal transfer printed with premium wax/resin ribbon for durability.

  2. 4"x6" MSL with RFID

    An EPC C1G2 RFD is embedded in the label, encoded per DFARS 252.211-7006 and tested 3 times. We track the RFID #'s for you and ensure there are no duplicates. We can also upload your Advance Ship Notice to WAWF, eliminating the frustration of manually typing 24 digit RFID #'s.

  3. uPrint-it Military Shipping label

    These PDF's of MIL-STD-129 compliant labels are emailed the day you purchase. Print with a laser printer and save a day and FedEx fees.

Exterior Container Label (ECL)

This is DoD's contents label. MIL-STD-129 requires one on every:

  • Shipping container
  • Palletized shipping container

EXPEDITER™ offers 3 MIL-STD-129 compliant ECL options:

  1. 4" x 6" Exterior Container Label

    Thermal transfer printed with premium wax/resin ribbon for durability. You can add a line of helpful info such as item size, RMA# or instructions

  2. 4"x6" Exterior Container Label with RFID

    An EPC C1G2 RFD is embedded in the label, encoded per DFARS 252.211-7006 and tested three times. We track the RFID #'s for you & ensure there are no duplicates. We can also upload your Advance Ship Notice to WAWF, eliminating the frustration of manually typing 24 digit RFID #'s.

  3. uPrint-it Exterior Container Label

    These PDF's of MIL-STD-129 compliant labels are emailed the day you purchase. Print with a laser printer ans save a day and FedEx fees.

Unit Container Label (UCL) and Intermediate Container Label (ICL)

These are DoD's contents labels for inside containers. MIL-STD-129 requires:

  • 1 UCL on each unit pack
  • 1 ICL on each intermediate container

EXPEDITER™ offers 3 MIL-STD-129 compliant ECL options:

  1. Standard 4"x2" - Most durable

    Sized for easy reading. Thermal transfer printed for high durability, just like the MSL and ECL.

  2. Small 2 1/2"x1 9/16" - Medium durable

    Sized to fit small containers and bags. Laser printed for use when abrasion AND weather exposure are minimal.

  3. uPrint-it PDF of Small 2 1/2"x1 9/16"

    These PDF's of MIL-STD-129 compliant labels are emailed the day you purchase. Print with a laser printer ans save a day and FedEx fees.
