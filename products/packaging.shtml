Slash cost, Do Mil-Spec packaging in-house

Eliminate high costs, long delays and double-handling! Bring military packaging in-house and win more DoD awards. GuidedPackaging™ uses your DoD contract to take the mystery out of military packaging.

We teach you how to read your contract, decipher it's MIL-STD-2073-1D codes and package your products with Mil-Spec materials. GuidedPackaging is a one-time session designed to make you self-sufficient. We use your contract as an example, but provide you with the knowledge to read any contract, look up MIL-STD-2073 codes, purchase Mil-Spec materials and package it.

Get started, call 617-314-7700

GuidedPackaging includes:

  1. Written report decoding your contract's MIL-STD-2073 packaging requirements* including Method of Preservation, Wrapping, Cushioning and Dunnage, Unit Containers, Intermediate Containers and Shipping Containers
  2. Bill of Materials for packaging your contract per MIL-STD-2073
  3. Two or more sources where you can purchase MIL-STD-2073 materials with confidence
  4. Step-by-Step Packaging Instructions for your contract
  5. List of MIL-STD-129 shipping labels required for your contract
  6. PDF of MIL-STD-2073-1D
  7. One hour phone session for instruction and Q&A

*Currently, we do NOT do contracts w/ wood, molded or modular containers

Additional services priced separately:

  1. EXPEDITER™ RFID labels with Advanced Ship Notices to WAWF
  2. EXPEDITER™ MIL-STD-129 labels
  3. EXPEDITER™ MIL-STD-130 UID labels
  4. Assistance with WAWF or IUID Registry
