EXPEDITER™ Stainless Steel UID labels are made of AMS 5513 Type 304 Stainless Steel annealed. CerMark™ is used to create text and barcode markings as durable as the metal itself.

  1. The surface of the stainless steel is prepared.
  2. CerMark™, a glass-ceramic enamel, is spray coated over the entire Stainless Steel surface and dried.
  3. A laser, tracing the text and barcodes, permanently fuses the CerMark™ to the Stainless Steel. Excess, un-fused CerMark™ is removed—leaving behind a high contrast, high resolution mark.
